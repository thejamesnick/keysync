## Philosophy
Track usage and adoption **without ever seeing secrets**.
All analytics are derived from **metadata events**.

## 1. Metrics Tracked (Zero-Knowledge)
*   **Accounts:** Total registered users.
*   **Projects:** Active project count.
*   **Blobs:** Number of encrypted bundles pushed.
*   **Activity:** Timestamps of pushes/pulls.
*   **Team Size:** Number of authorized keys per project.

**NOT Tracked:**
*   Secret names or values.
*   File contents.
*   Environment variable specific data.

## 3. Storage
*   **Events Table:** `timestamp`, `event_type`, `account_id` (hashed), `project_id`.
*   **Aggregates:** Daily/Weekly summaries to avoid storing massive event logs.
